Features

Everything your LLC needs

CLI on your machine, API in the cloud, web dashboard everywhere.

📄

Invoice & Payments

Create invoices with Stripe payment links. Auto-payment reminders. Share a "pay now" link via text or email.

🏦

Automated Bank Feeds

Connect via Plaid. Transactions are fetched, categorized, and imported into your ledger automatically.

📸

Receipt OCR

Snap a receipt photo. Extract merchant, date, total, and line items. Auto-categorize and append to your ledger.

💰

Tax Estimation

Self-employment tax + income tax + state tax. Quarterly payment suggestions with safe harbor calculations.

⏱️

Time Tracking

Pull hours from Toggl or Clockify. Create invoice-ready summaries by client and project.

🔔

Notifications

Desktop and email alerts for tax deadlines, unpaid invoices, and ledger health. Runs daily from cron.
