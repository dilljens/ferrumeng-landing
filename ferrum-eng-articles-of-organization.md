## After Filing

1. Download the stamped Articles of Organization (PDF) from WyoBiz
2. You'll get a **SOS File Number** — save this, you'll need it for bank accounts
3. Filing fee: **$100** (paid online by credit/debit card + ~2.4% convenience fee)

## Next: Get EIN from IRS

| **Address** | Your Sheridan address |
| **Reason** | Started a new business |
